WHY I WANT TO VISIT SOUTH KOREA

           South Korea is well-known for its sophisticated technology and imaginative design, and I'd like to witness some of the most recent innovations in person. South Korea's natural beauty, such as its mountains and beaches, is extremely appealing to me. I'd want to go trekking and enjoy the great outdoors. Plus, I'm a fan of K-pop and Korean dramas and would want to see some of the filming places or witness a live performance. Finally, I want to learn about South Korea's distinct culture and traditions, such as sampling traditional Korean food and learning about the country's history.
