WHY I WANT TO VISIT NETHERLANDS

           I'd like to go to the Netherlands to see the beautiful tulip fields and windmills. The vivid hues of the tulips combined with the distinctive windmills create a stunning picture. Being a lover of art and history, I would be drawn to the Netherlands because of its rich cultural past. There is no shortage of intriguing places to visit in Amsterdam, from the world-renowned artwork of Van Gogh and Rembrandt to the medieval architecture. The Netherlands is also known for its cycling culture, and as someone who enjoys being healthy, I would love to cycle across the country's lovely countryside.
