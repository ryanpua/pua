Bucket list

-    Take a solo road trip. I want to experience traveling alone, i feel like it is a great way to recharge and explore at my own pace. Explore the open road, stopping at scenic spots along the way.

-    Explore a new city or country. I love people-watching and soaking up the ambiance of a new place, while taking a walking tour of a city or neighborhood. This can be a great way to discover hidden gems and get a sense of the local culture.

-    Go stargazing. I love spending time in nature, there's nothing quite like a clear night sky full of stars. Camping in a remote location or finding a local stargazing group to join.

-    Learn a new language. For a trip to a foreign country or simply to challenge myself intellectually, learning a new language can be a fun and fulfilling experience.

-    Attend a music festival or concert.

-    Try a new cuisine. Food is a universal language, and trying new dishes can be a great way to explore different cultures and expand my culinary horizons.

-    Take a class or workshop. Whether it's cooking, painting, or photography, taking a class or workshop can be a great way to learn new skills and meet like-minded individuals.

-    Graduate college. Excitement, bittersweet, pride, anxiousness, and love. We can only imagine the feelings bubbling through our minds. What am I gonna feel when I graduate?

-    Learn a new instrument or form of art. Whether painting, sculpture, or music, exploring a new form of art can be a great way to express myself and tap into creativity.

-    Visit a natural wonder.

-    Take a hot air balloon ride. Seeing the world from a new perspective can be a magical experience, and a hot air balloon ride can offer a unique and memorable way to do just that.
